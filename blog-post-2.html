Ethics in Healthcare Data

Published on April 25, 2025

Handling healthcare data requires balancing innovation with ethical responsibility. Issues like privacy, bias, and fairness are critical in data science.

Key Ethical Considerations

  • Privacy: Ensuring patient data is anonymized and secure.
  • Bias: Addressing biases in datasets that could lead to unfair outcomes.
  • Transparency: Making ML models interpretable to clinicians.

Best Practices

Adopt frameworks like GDPR, use differential privacy, and involve diverse teams to mitigate bias.

Conclusion

Ethical data science in healthcare builds trust and ensures equitable care for all.
